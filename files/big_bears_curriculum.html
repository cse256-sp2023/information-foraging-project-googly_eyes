Curriculum in the Big Bear Classroom

ABC Books

To celebrate the end of our ABC unit, we savored alphabet soup - of course! - and Alphabit cereal (who knew it was still around?!). Big Bears discovered something special in the precious pockets of their ABC books, and then put their problem-solving and teamwork skills to good use at circle. You will have to ask your Big Bears more about that!

Chicks!

Next up we will dive into our chick unit! Big Bears have already begun charting the growth of our 16 chicks in their personal chick folders, marking off each day of growth as they walk into the classroom. We will continue to use our chick folders to not only chart growth, but also sketch growth, do fun activities, and learn new chick development terms. *If you are interested in adopting a chick, please let us know by Monday, April 22. Ms. Suzy was a chick mom for years, so if you have any questions, please ask!
